*   **Synthesis Procedures:**

    *   **CuZSM SA (Ion Exchange):**
        1.  Disperse 1 g of ZSM-5 zeolite in 50 ml ethanol under magnetic stirring.
        2.  Separately, ultrasonically dissolve 10 mg of CuSO₄·5H₂O in 50 ml deionized water.
        3.  Add the CuSO₄ solution to the ZSM-5 suspension.
        4.  Stir vigorously for 12 hours.
        5.  Centrifuge the mixture to collect the solid product.
        6.  Wash the solid thoroughly with deionized water and ethanol, followed by centrifugation after each wash.
        7.  Dry the washed solid in an oven at 60 °C to remove residual solvents.
        8.  Grind the dried product into a fine powder.

    *   **CuZSM SACL (Calcination of CuZSM SA):**
        1.  Place the finely ground CuZSM SA powder in a porcelain boat.
        2.  Heat the sample in a 5% H₂/Ar atmosphere with a controlled heating rate of 5 °C min⁻¹ up to 550 °C.
        3.  Hold the sample at 550 °C for 6 hours.
        4.  Allow the sample to cool naturally to room temperature.

    *   **CuZSM CLNP (Solid-State Mixing & Calcination):**
        1.  Mechanically mix 1 g of ZSM-5 zeolite and 1 mg of CuSO₄·5H₂O by thorough grinding.
        2.  Place the mixture in a reactor (implied porcelain boat).
        3.  Heat the mixture in a 5% H₂/Ar atmosphere with a controlled heating rate of 5 °C min⁻¹ up to 550 °C.
        4.  Hold the mixture at 550 °C for 6 hours.
        5.  Allow the sample to cool naturally to room temperature.
        6.  Wash the resulting solid thoroughly with deionized water and ethanol, followed by centrifugation after each wash.
        7.  Dry the washed solid overnight in an oven at 60 °C.
